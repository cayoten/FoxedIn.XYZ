FoxedIn Links & Forms Hub

Welcome to the hub for all links FoxedIn! FoxedIn is a fast-growing, fully featured network which offers Discord, Telegram, and Minecraft communities. If helping out in a fast-paced environment sounds fun, why not join us? Moderators help keep the chat clean and tidy and will enforce actions on any rule breakers. If you want to help host and run fun, exciting and new events, the Event Team is for you! If you're looking to get unbanned from our servers, you'll need the Ban Appeal form. If you want to prove that you're a real artist and be able to post art on our network, go to the Verified Artist form. If you're looking to give us feedback on how we're doing, what we should improve, and general other things, check out our Feedback Form! You can find all links below.
